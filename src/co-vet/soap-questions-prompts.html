Prompts & SOAP Questions

Open this section during exams/recording in CoVet. AI is prompt oriented, and these questions (or similar) can help us assess as many details as possible about the Pt. This section will be updated as we improve our prompts/questions and customize Co-Vet, so please check in for updates.

Exam Intro/Prompt

Start each appointment by greeting the Client and patient, and verify nature of visit. Explain to O that we are now integrating AI into our workflow, and we are using an audio-based, scribe-type software to assist creating our medical records, and verify consent. Once ok, START RECORDING, and say... (example)...

"TODAY WE HAVE [patient name], HERE FOR [appointment type (recheck ears, annual, etc)]. [patient name] IS A [age], [f/m, s/n], [species], and CURRENT WEIGHT TODAY IS ___."

Start SOAP questions from here, making sure to specifically state "NO VOMITING, NO DIARRHEA, etc (keywords/prompts matter!)"...
